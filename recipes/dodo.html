Local Nigerian food

Dodo is a popular food that originated from the western part of Nigeria.

It is typical to the Yoruba culture. Very sweet and tasty

Ingredients

  • Plantain
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Dice the plantain into cubes or fingers
  2. Sprinkle a pinch of salt in it and few drops of water to dissolve the salt
  3. Fry it in the hot oil till it turns brown
  4. Serve hot and yummy
