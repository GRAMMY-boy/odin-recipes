Efo Riro

Local Nigerian Soup

Efo riro is a vegetable soup that originated from the western part of Nigeria.

The soup is typical to the Yoruba culture.

Ingredients

  • vegetables
  • Assorted Meat
  • Palm Oil
  • Spices
  • red bell Pepper
  • Locust beans
  • Cray fish

Steps

  1. Shred the vegetables
  2. Add a little palm oil to the pot, fry the meat alongside the spices and all other Ingredients
  3. Leave the stock to seamer for about 15 mins
  4. Lastly, add the shrreded vegetables. It should cook for another 10 mins
  5. Serve hot with or without Amala
