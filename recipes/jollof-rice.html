Jollof Rice

Local Nigerian Rice Dish

Jollof rice is a popupar dish that originated from Nigeria.

Jollof rice is typical to every culture in Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Pepper
  • Tomatos
  • Spices
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Blend the pepper, tomatos, and onions
  2. perboil the quantity of rice you want to cook
  3. Fry the blended pepper with oil for about 15 mins
  4. Add the perboiled rice to the fried sauce alongside othe ingredients.
  5. Serve hot with a chilled drink
